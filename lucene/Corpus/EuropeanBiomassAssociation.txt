european biomass association
the european biomass association aebiom from the official french name association europ enne pour la biomasse is a european trade association opens to national biomass associations and bioenergy companies active in europe aebiom was founded in 1990 and aims to promote biomass production and application throughout europe 
aebiom is the umbrella organisation of the european pellet council epc the european industry of pellet suppliers eips and the international biomass torrefaction council ibtc
